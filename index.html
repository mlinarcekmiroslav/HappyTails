PREVENTIVE CARE AND DENTISTRY

It is not a secret that an ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure.

To keep your pet's good health for years to come, we offer a wide range of preventive services that include but are not limited to:


  • Thorough snout-to-tail exams
  • Vaccination
  • Parasite prevention
  • Tick and flea control
  • Annual lab analytics
  • Dental maintenance
  • Ear and skin care
  • Nutritional evaluation
  • Grooming

Prevention has an essential role in fighting disease occurrence. It helps both you and your pet stay safe and happy.

DIAGNOSTICS

Every pet owner knows their pet by heart. Even the slightest change in mood or behavior indicates the pet is going through something.

In such situations, it is necessary to perform diagnostics to determine the root of the problem and assign adequate treatment:



  • Ultrasound
  • X-ray
  • Complete laboratory analysis

SURGERY

Presurgical testing and preparation for surgery are of great importance since it significantly affects its course and outcome.

Whether it is about a minor or an advanced surgical procedure, our skilled and specialized veterinary staff is here to address your pet's needs most efficiently and least stressful way possible.

We are happy to answer all your questions and help guide you through treatment and recovery.
